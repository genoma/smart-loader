Wait For Images

a jQuery 1.9.X Plugin

The loader plugin

I've seen plenty of times a lot of people asking on many websites "how can I add a function that shows a page only after all images are loaded?", and while working on a very long and complex project, i've decided to develop a small function that does exactly that: waiting for all images that are completely loaded, than show the page.
